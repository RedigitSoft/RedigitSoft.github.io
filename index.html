About Redigit

A small studio that cares about the details.

Redigit Softwares Private Limited is an independent software studio focused on cross-platform product engineering. We’re small on purpose: fewer handoffs, senior people on the work, and a bias toward simple, durable solutions over buzzwords.

The studio was founded by Akshay Sharma — a software architect, open-source maintainer, and Kotlin Multiplatform developer. A lot of what we build ends up open source, because we think the best way to earn trust is to show the work.
